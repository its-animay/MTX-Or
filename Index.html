MTX-O device  

IOT based hardware for real time tracking using high efficient sensors for accurate measure MTX-O module nterface: USB Type-B, Wi-Fi, Baud Rate: 115200 (default, can not be changed) Voltage: 3.3-5V, built-in rechargeable battery (1200mAh capacity, 3.7V) Output Content: 3-axis Acceleration+Angle+Angular Velocity Range: Acceleration (±16g), Gyroscope (±2000°/s), Angle (X, Z-axis: ±180°, Y ±90°) Resolution: Acceleration (0.005g), Gyroscope (0.61°/s) Angle Accuracy(after calibrated): X, Y-axis: 0.05° (Static) , X, Y-axis: 0.1° (Dynamic)
